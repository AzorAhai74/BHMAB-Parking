Self-Parking

Self-Parking is $10/Day and you may enter an exit the lot for this flat,daily fee. Call the hotel on the day of your arrival at 205-703-9920 to verify your reservation information to receive the code to the gate to park. Once you park, enter the door in the back of the building and walk up to the front desk to check in. We will then issue you the parking pass to put on your dash.

Select Self-Parking

Valet Parking

Valet parking is $20/Day and we will park and provide your vehicle for you when you need it from our gated lot behind the hotel. Pull into the front circle off 20th street to check-in. Our valet attendants will register your vehicle and provide you with a claim ticket. Call down to the front desk when you need your vehicle with the number on your claim ticket.
